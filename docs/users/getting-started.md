# Getting Started Guide

Welcome to Duet Company's AI-first data infrastructure platform. This guide will help you get up and running in minutes.

## Prerequisites

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Basic understanding of data concepts
- Your data source (CSV, database, API)

## Quick Start (5 minutes)

### 1. Sign Up

Visit [https://aidatalabs.ai/signup](https://aidatalabs.ai/signup) and create your account.

Choose a plan:
- **Starter:** $999/month - 1TB data, 5 users
- **Growth:** $2,999/month - 10TB data, 20 users  
- **Enterprise:** Custom - Unlimited scale

### 2. Describe Your Needs

Once logged in, you'll see a chat interface. Simply describe what you need:

```
I need an e-commerce analytics platform to track:
- Daily sales and revenue
- Customer acquisition and retention
- Product performance
- Marketing campaign effectiveness
```

Our AI agents will automatically:
- Design the optimal database schema
- Provision infrastructure
- Set up dashboards and reports
- Configure data pipelines

### 3. Upload Your Data

Connect your data source:
- **Database:** PostgreSQL, MySQL, ClickHouse, Snowflake
- **Files:** CSV, JSON, Parquet (up to 100MB)
- **API:** REST or GraphQL endpoints
- **Streaming:** Kafka, Kinesis, Pulsar

### 4. Start Querying

Ask questions in plain English:

```
Show me revenue trends for the last 6 months
What are our top 10 products by sales?
How has customer retention changed over time?
```

Get instant visualizations and insights.
